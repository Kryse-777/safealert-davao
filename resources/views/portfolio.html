My Photoshop works

I began to use photoshop from our class subject and project submission, I took interest to the software and the medium that is digital art. As my hobby I now use photoshop to hone my skills at altering and creating images from ideas that storm into my head. These works are generally from pop culture media, as my go to kinds of photos and sketch pieces that I like to work on.

Most of my inspiration and style of creating images usually veer to the genre of science fiction, I like the concept of the combination of reality and a likely possible reality which means that the potential ideas of the works that I make and can make are virtually endless.
